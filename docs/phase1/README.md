# Phase 1: World Design

Phase 1 creates the complete world design, including lore, geography, regions, cities, and dungeons.

## Sections

### 1.1 Lore & Setting Foundation (~100 tasks)

#### 1.1.1 Creation Myth & History
- World creation myth
- Era definitions (primordial, ancient, classical, modern)
- World history timeline
- Current conflict
- Player's role

#### 1.1.2 World Overview
- World overview document
- Elevator pitch
- World name
- Scale and geography

#### 1.1.3 Factions (6 factions × 9 tasks each)
- Name, overview, goals, values
- Territory, leadership, aesthetics
- History, key NPCs

#### 1.1.4 Faction Relationships
- Relationship matrix
- 15 faction pair relationships
- Player faction mechanics
- Reputation system

#### 1.1.5 World Rules & Systems
- Magic system
- Technology level
- Religions/deities
- Knowledge systems

#### 1.1.6 Visual References
- World mood board
- Architecture, landscape, character, technology mood boards
- Faction mood boards

### 1.2 World Map Design (~80 tasks)

#### 1.2.1 Continental Layout
- Continent shape
- Map dimensions
- Oceans, mountains, rivers, forests, deserts
- Digital world map

#### 1.2.2 Biome Definition (9 biomes × 6 tasks each)
- Temperate Forest, Desert, Tundra
- Swamp, Volcanic, Coastal
- Underground, Magical/Corrupted, Plains

#### 1.2.3 Region Planning
- Total regions (~13)
- Region names and boundaries
- Biome and faction assignments
- Level ranges

#### 1.2.4 Travel & Connectivity
- Road networks
- Sea travel
- Fast travel system
- Gated content

### 1.3 Region Detail Design (~200 tasks)

13 regions × 15 tasks each:
- Name, description, theme, lore
- Map layout
- Cities/towns, dungeons, points of interest
- Resources, monsters, NPCs, quests
- Music theme, mood board
- Travel connections

### 1.4 Cities & Towns (~200 tasks)

20 settlements × 10 tasks each:
- Description, purpose, layout
- Buildings, NPCs, shops, services
- Lore, architecture, music

### 1.5 Dungeons & Points of Interest (~330 tasks)

30 dungeons × 11 tasks each:
- Description, theme, entry requirements
- Layout, difficulty, monsters
- Boss(es), loot/rewards
- Puzzles/mechanics, lore, music

## Total: ~900 tasks

## Key Deliverables

1. **Lore Bible**
   - Creation myth
   - World history
   - Faction documentation
   - World rules

2. **World Map**
   - Continental map
   - Biome distribution
   - Region boundaries
   - Travel routes

3. **Region Design Documents** (13 documents)
   - Detailed map
   - Content lists
   - Design specifications

4. **City/Town Design Documents** (20 documents)
   - Layout maps
   - NPC rosters
   - Service lists

5. **Dungeon Design Documents** (30 documents)
   - Floor layouts
   - Encounter designs
   - Reward tables

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Complete lore bible approved
- [ ] World map finalized
- [ ] All regions fully designed
- [ ] All cities/towns fully designed
- [ ] All dungeons fully designed
- [ ] Ready to begin Phase 2
